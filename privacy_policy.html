Privacy Policy for Momotaro

Effective Date: October 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Momotaro ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information we receive from third-party food delivery services, such as Uber Eats, when you place an order with our restaurant.

Our primary commitment is to process your order efficiently while respecting your privacy and protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

When an order is placed with our restaurant through a delivery partner, we receive the information necessary to prepare and fulfill that order. This information may include:

  • Customer Name: To identify the order.
  • Order Details: The specific food and beverage items you have ordered.
  • Special Instructions: Any specific requests or allergy information you provide.
  • Delivery Address and Contact Information: As provided by the delivery partner for fulfillment purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect strictly for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: To accurately prepare your food and beverage order as requested.
  • Internal Operations: To display order details on our internal kitchen dashboard system to manage the preparation process.
  • Customer Service: To address any issues or questions related to a specific order.

We do not use your personal information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than fulfilling your order.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Delivery Partners: To coordinate order fulfillment and delivery.
  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect the personal information we receive. Access to order information is restricted to restaurant staff who need it to perform their job of preparing and managing orders.

6. Data Retention

We retain order information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the order and for our internal business and accounting records.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
